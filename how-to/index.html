Connect your wallet

Sign into your NvestBank wallet and confirm that you have ETH in your account.

Wrap ETH

Once you connect the NvestBank wallet you need to wrap the ETH you wish to exchange for set tokens.

Enable Tokens & Trade

After you give permission to the ERC20 Smart Contracts to move your tokens. You simple place the order for the trade which you wish to partake in.

Market Orders

Select the market order tab on top of the order handler.

Enter the amout you would like to buy or sell.

Market orders will batch orders on the book into one transaction.

Hit 'Buy' or 'Sell' to complete transaction.

Limit Orders

Select the limit order tab on top of the order handler.

Choose Buy or Sell.

Enter the amout you would like to buy or sell.

Determine desired price.

Hit 'place order' and sign the transaction.

Your signed order is now on the orderbook marked with the wallet icon.
